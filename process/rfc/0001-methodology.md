## The political vision in full — the six theses (`T#`)

> Register 3. Engine room, not lyrics — enacted in the crossings, the license, the forgery. Full text + references: [`../political-vision.md`](../political-vision.md). Comment thesis by thesis (e.g. *"T4: …"*).

- **T1 — Authenticity is a power relation, and we forge to prove it.** "The real" is certified by gatekeepers (labels, Discogs, critics, the market). A credible forgery that enters the register shows authenticity is an *effect of certification, not an essence*. We attack the **criterion of the real**, not a record. *(Debord; Benjamin.)*
- **T2 — Against enclosure, for the commons.** AI models privatize humanity's collective musical culture and resell it. The engine is free (AGPL): a **commons that resists enclosure**. What was made by all must not be re-privatized by a few. *(Free software; Hyde, the gift.)*
- **T3 — Creolization, not smoothie.** Crossing genres is either **creolization** (Glissant — the unpredictable collision that produces the genuinely new) or **flattening** (the market smoothie, all difference dissolved into frictionless content = slop). Keep the friction, keep the human ear, throw out the slop. *(Glissant, Poetics of Relation.)*
- **T4 — The right to opacity, against total legibility.** *(the heart.)* The spectacle / the AI / the database demand everything be legible, indexable, extractable. Glissant's **right to opacity**: cultures may stay opaque, non-extractable. We defend it by producing the unclassifiable — illegibility as resistance. The 360k aren't a stream to sell; they're a **flood that saturates the register**. *(Glissant; Scott.)*
- **T5 — No clean outside, so the work implicates itself.** We use extractive AI, court investors, eye a prestigious conference. There is **no exterior** (Debord). Honesty = operating *inside* the contradiction and naming it: the critique of extraction is itself an extraction, named. *(See [`../../GENESIS.md`](../../GENESIS.md).)*
- **T6 — Meaning against content, the human ear as a stance.** The spectacle's end point is infinite frictionless content. Defending the **irreducibly human** — taste, the ear, the 2 a.m. surprise — affirms culture as *lived meaning, not extractable throughput*.

**Positive synthesis:** Le Malentendu defends **creolization** and the **right to opacity**, against **enclosure** and **flattening** — with the enemy's weapons, and owning it.
